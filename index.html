About Us

Rooted in Agriculture, Driven by Service

Since 1988, Network Trading LLC has been a trusted partner in the bulk commodities industry. Located in Nerstrand, Minnesota — approximately 50 miles south of Minneapolis — we connect producers with the quality feed ingredients they need to succeed.

Our experienced team works closely with farms, feed mills, and pet food manufacturers across the country, delivering reliable supply chains and competitive pricing on a comprehensive range of agricultural commodities.
